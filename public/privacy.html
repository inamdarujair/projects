📋 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

🔍 Information We Collect

AUTORIG is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our PC building services:

  • Component Preferences: Your selected PC components and build configurations
  • Budget Information: Your specified budget range for build suggestions
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information for compatibility

🛡️ How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized PC build recommendations
  • Validate component compatibility
  • Improve our algorithm and user experience
  • Generate performance estimates

🔐 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data is stored locally on your device when possible
  • Encrypted connections for all data transmission
  • No personal identification information is collected
  • Regular security audits and updates

📤 Data Sharing

AUTORIG does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for research purposes only.

🍪 Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Maintaining your session
  • Improving site performance

👤 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your build configurations

📞 Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our Support page.

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
